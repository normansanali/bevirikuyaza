Katherine Macgregor Net Worth

Katherine Macgregor was born on January 12, 1925 in Glendale, CA. She is best known for her role as Harriet Oleson in the NBC drama Little House on the Prairie from 1974 to 1983, providing comic relief to the show. After her time on the show, she went on to become a local theater actress.
