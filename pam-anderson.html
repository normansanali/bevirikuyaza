Pamela Anderson News - Us Weekly

Name:Pamela Anderson

Relationship:Single

Birthday:July 1, 1967

About

Born in British Columbia, Canada, Pamela Anderson has made a career out of being large-breasted, showing up in Playboy and porn videos, and being famously married to – and divorced from – Motley Crue drummer Tommy Lee.

The blonde bombshell got her start as a Labatts spokeswoman, which led to a spread in Playboy. Anderson skyrocketed to fame with her role as CJ Parker in Baywatch.

Known for her rollercoaster love life, Anderson has been married to Tommy Lee, Kid Rock, and Rick Soloman and has had relationships with a myriad of men, including Fred Durst, Ben Affleck, Scott Baio and Brett Michaels.

Anderson and Lee had a difficult relationship, which included abuse, a sex tape, Anderson’s contraction of Hepatitis C, and two children.

Anderson’s newest small screen venture — a reality series titled Pam: Girl on the Loose — premiered in August 2008 on E!.

In March 2010, Anderson competed in the tenth season of Dancing With The Stars with partner DAMIAN WHITEWOOD.
