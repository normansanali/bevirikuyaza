Kyle Busch Biography

Kyle Busch was born on 2nd May 1985 in Las Vegas, Nevada in the United States.

Kyle Busch’s height is 6 feet 1 inch. His weight is 84 kg.

He races for the NASCAR Cup Series, for Joe Gibbs Racing, and part-time for the NASCAR Camping World Truck Series. He also rides for Kyle Busch Motorsports.

Kyle Busch started his career in driving in 1998 after his 13th birthday and won several races from 1999 to 2001 in legends car racing. He started competing in NASCAR in 2003 and went on to win several titles and championships. 

Kyle Busch has made a whopping net worth which is $80 million. His salary is $9.6 million.

His parents are Tom Busch (father) and Gaye Busch (mother). His father controlled the gas pedal as Kyle drove the vehicle. His father used to work as a mechanic at a Ford dealership and was a tools dealer.

Kyle Busch has a brother named Kurt Busch who is an American professional auto racing driver. Kurt is the 2004 NASCAR Cup Series champion and 2017 Daytona 500 winner.

Kyle Busch is married to Samantha Sarcinella in December 2010 in Chicago. 

She is a native of St. John, Indiana. She graduated from Purdue University with a psychology degree.

NASCAR legend Kyle Busch’s wife, Samantha Busch, is much more than a trophy wife (although she did appear on the reality show Racing Wives).

She’s an entrepreneur, a mother to two children, an IVF advocate, an author, a philanthropist, and a lifestyle blogger.

The Busch family calls Lake Norman, North Carolina home, where they own a 13,000-square-foot house.

Samantha owns a clothing company and online boutique, Shop Samantha Busch, with race-day-themed outfits and accessories.
