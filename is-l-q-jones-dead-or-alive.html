Is L.Q. Jones dead or alive?

L. Q. Jones was an American actor and director who appeared in Sam Peckinpah’s films Ride the High Country, Major Dundee, The Wild Bunch, The Ballad of Cable Hogue, and Pat Garrett and Billy the Kid. His later film roles include Casino, The Patriot, The Mask of Zorro, and A Prairie Home Companion.

In this article, we will find out whether he is still alive or dead as we dive into his personal life to know more about him. If he is indeed dead, we will find out his cause of death, and other information about his death.

Is L.Q. Jones dead or alive?

L.Q. Jones is the son of Jessie Paralee McQueen and Justice Ellis McQueen. He was born to his parents in Beaumont, Texas, the United States on August 19, 1927, but sadly passed away on Saturday, July 9, 2022, according to the report. 

The American actor and director is said to have died at the age of 94. Jones, according to his grandson Erté deGarces died of natural causes.

He was a father of three children. Marlin Jones, Marilyn Jones, and Stephen Jones are the children of L. Q. Jones.
