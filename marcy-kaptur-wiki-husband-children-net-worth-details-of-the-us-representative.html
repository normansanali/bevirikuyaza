Marcy Kaptur Wikipedia Explored

Marcy Kaptur has a dedicated Wikipedia page of her own as she is one of the famous faces in American politics.

Marcy’s full name is Marcia Carolyn Kaptur. She was born on 17th June 1946 in Toledo, Ohio, United States, and is 75 years old as of 2022.

Furthermore, her mother and father are Anastasia Delores (Rogowski) and Stephen Jacob Kaptur. Both of her parents were of Polish descent.

Anastasia, Marcy’s mother, was an automobile union organizer. Her family operated a small grocery. When she was 13, Marcy started volunteering with the Ohio Democratic Party.

Talking about her education, Kaptur graduated from St Ursula Academy in 1964. Marcy was the first person in her family to attend college. Later in 1968, she received her undergraduate degree from the University of Wisconsin–Madison and, in 1974, a Master of Urban Planning.

In addition, Marcy even did her doctoral studies in urban planning development finance at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. She received her doctorate in 1981.
