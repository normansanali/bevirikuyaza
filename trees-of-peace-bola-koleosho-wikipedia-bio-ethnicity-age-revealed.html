Trees Of Peace: Bola Koleosho Wikipedia Bio Ethnicity &amp; Age Revealed

The recent Netflix release “Trees of Peace” actor Bola Koleosho has earned the praise of the fans and the critics likewise, and fans want to know more about the actor and her background.

Bola is the actor who portrays the role of Mutesi in the award-winning picture Trees of Peace. With the Netflix release of the show, the actress and the film have gained huge attention and exposure.

Set in 1994, during the Genocide of the Tutsi in Rwanda, Bola, alongside Eliane Umuhre, Charmaine Bingwa, and Ella Cannon, portrayed a surreal recreation of the incident impressing the audience and the fans.

Who Is Bola Koleosho? Wikipedia Bio

Bola Koleosho is a Nigerian American actress who starred in the recent Netflix Original movie Trees of Peace.

The movie was originally only shown at Film Festivals around the world but was later bought by Netflix.

With its release on Netflix on June 10, fans are curious about the cast. Bola has also gotten quite the exposure from the movie, along with the other cast members.

Apart from this award-winning flick, Koleosho has also starred in the comedy-drama show Dear White People, all about a diverse group of students navigating through racial discrimination at a predominantly white Ivy League College.

The actress has also worked as a senior coordinator in Warner Bros. Entertainment and started acting in 2020. She is currently the Chief Executive Officer of BOLA BEAUTY and also a freelance writer.

Bola is active on her Twitter and Instagram and has recently started uploading videos on her Youtube Channel as well. The young actress is also seen doing sponsorships on her social media.

What Is Bola Koleosho Ethnicity?

Bola Koleosho belongs to the Nigerian American ethnic group. Her father came to America, specifically Texas, from Nigeria in 1985 to fulfill his American dream.

So the actress was born in Nigeria but came to America with her dad and gained American citizenship.

She was raised by her single father along with her siblings, as he started his own business with a $200 loan.

With her background roots in Nigeria, the actress always helps out fellow black artists, even with just a shoutout on her social media.

How Old Is Bola Koleosho? Age Of Trees Of Peace Actress

Born on February 13, 1997, Bola Koleosho is 25 years old.

The actress was born in her home country Nigeria, but later at the age of 12, she moved to Texas with her father.

She was raised by a single dad and finished high school in Minnesota. Later she graduated from the University of Minnesota with a Bachelor’s Degree in General Physiology at 21 as her father wanted her to be a doctor.

However, the Tree of Peace actress wanted to be an artist, so she earned her New York University Performing Arts Industry Essentials Certificate from Yellowbrick recently.
