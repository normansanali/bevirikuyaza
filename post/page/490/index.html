How do you tell if a cat has Down's syndrome?

published on July 17, 2024
A so-called "Down syndrome cat" typically manifests some distinctive characteristics, including: Broad noses.Upturned eyes (which may be set widely apart)Small or unusual ears.Low muscle tone.Difficulty walking.Difficulty with elimination (urination or defecation)Hearing or vision loss.Heart problems. How do you know if your pet has Down syndrome?
