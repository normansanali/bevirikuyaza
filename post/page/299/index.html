Session Drummer Tony Thompson Dead at 48

published on August 16, 2024
(CelebrityAccess News Service) — Top session drummer Tony Thompson, who also manned the skins for disco band Chic, died November 12 of renal cancer in Los Angeles. He was 48 After Chic disbanded in 1981, Thompson became a much sought-after percussionist for a diverse group of artists including David Bowie, Mike Jagger, Madonna, Rod Stewart, and Led Zeppelin at Live Aid.
