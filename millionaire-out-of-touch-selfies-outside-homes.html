Im a multi-millionaire at 23 &amp; own four houses - trolls say Im out of touch when I pose for se

A SELF-MADE multi-millionaire has revealed that she picks her next property purchase based on whether she can get a good selfie.

Linsey Donavan, 23, didn't have much money growing up in Maryland but has since established a property portfolio worth over $7 million dollars.

Linsey, who made her fortune by streaming videos on social media decided to invest the money into property.

As well as spending her money wisely Linsey also loves to splurge on high-end items.

Linsey told Truly that she likes to treat herself from time to time – and has splashed out a staggering £391k on cars and has also spent £900 on a collar and leash for her pooch.

The multi-millionaire recently took to Instagram to show that she was on the hunt for a new property.

Now the young millionaire lives in a plush $2m mansion that's so big her dogs have their own bedrooms.
