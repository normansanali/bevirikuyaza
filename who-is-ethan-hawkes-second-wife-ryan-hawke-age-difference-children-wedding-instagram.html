Ethan Green Hawke is an American actor and film director. Owing to his successful career, he has been nominated for four Academy Awards, two Golden Globe Awards, and a Tony Award.

This article will not focus on his career at large. We are instead going to take a closer look into his family.

Who is Ethan Hawke’s second wife Ryan Hawke? Age difference, children, wedding, Instagram

Hollywood is famous for the marriages between its stars. However, it is equally infamous for the number of divorces it has. Ethan Hawke is not free from this as the actor has been married twice.

His first marriage was to actress Uma Thurman on May 1, 1998. They met two years earlier on the set of Gattaca in 1996. Their marriage was blessed with two children. Their first daughter, Maya, was born on July 8, 1998, and their second child, a son named Levon, was born in 2002.

A year after his birth, they separated in 2003 amid allegations of Hawke’s infidelity. The next year, they officially filed for divorce.

After their divorce was finalized, Hawke remarried Ryan Shawhughes, who had briefly nannied his children with Thurman. They have welcomed two daughters.
