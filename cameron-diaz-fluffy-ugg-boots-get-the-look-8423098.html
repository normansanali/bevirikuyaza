Cameron Diazs Cozy, Fluffy Boots Are Almost Sold Out

Cameron Diaz was recently spotted out in Santa Barbara wearing one of the most laid-back outfits I’ve seen in a while — and I loved it. The actress wore an oversized denim jacket, gray sweatshirt, and matching lounge pants. She accessorized with a wide-brim hat, Prada belt bag, and sleek black frames. Though, I’m choosing to focus my attention on her totally cozy boots. 

Ugg Mini Uggbraid Boot

Diaz donned the Mini Uggbraid style from Ugg, which featured stitched details, a thick platform sole, and a warm brown hue. The key component? A plush-front lining that wrapped around the ankle. It almost looked as if the boot had been folded over, giving the footwear a snuggly look. Unfortunately, the star’s identical shoes are almost sold out — but I found nine similar cloud-like styles all on Amazon, starting at just 30.    

9 Cozy Foldover Boots

Ugg Ultra Mini Braid Fashion Boot

There couldn’t be a more perfect lookalike. These Ugg Ultra Mini Braid boots encapsulate the same essence as the original shoes, boasting a thin vertical stitching pattern, lug sole, chestnut hue, and — of course — a fluffy collar. One shopper said they receive compliments on these shoes “every time” they wear them, while another claims the boots are “stylish, comfortable, and warm.” Best of all, the Ultra Mini boots are 33 percent off right now. 

Koolaburra by Ugg Aubrei Mini Boot

Koolaburra by Ugg does a great job at offering shoppers a Diaz-inspired footwear option — especially at an affordable price. For $65, enjoy the Aubrei mini boot, which includes a suede upper, pull tab, and faux fur shaft. While the color is slightly lighter than the original, the overall aesthetic is the same. Shoppers say they “feel like slippers all day” and claim the “sole traction is lightweight and perfect when walking on snow.”

European Softest Faux Fur Slipper Bootie

If you’re looking for a real bargain deal, the European Softest faux fur slipper bootie is the way to go. This pick toes the line between slipper and bootie, bringing a memory foam insole, soft collar, and easy slip-on design to the forefront. The rubber sole is also durable and sturdy, which makes them last. With over 1,200 five-star ratings, this slipper bootie has received more than a few stamps of approval. “They are the coziest, warmest slippers I have ever owned,” said a reviewer. Another chimed in by saying it’s like “walking on clouds,” adding in that they’re “heavenly.”
