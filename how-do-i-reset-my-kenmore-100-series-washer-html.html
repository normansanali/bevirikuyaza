How do I reset my Kenmore 100 Series washer?

Answer

Most washer’s can be reset like a personal computer. Unplug or switch the circuit breaker off for 30 seconds. Plug the power cable into the wall outlet or turn the breaker back on and wait 30 seconds. Press the Pause / Cancel / Stop button twice to clear the display.

People sometimes question, how do you reset a Kenmore washing machine?

How to Reset a Kenmore Elite HE Washer

Press the “Stop” button twice, and then press the “Power” button.

Choose a wash cycle (e.g. “Normal”), and then choose your wash choices (e.g. “Hot/Cold” or “Cold/Cold”).

Press the “Start” button to finish the reset procedure and restart the washing machine.

Does Kenmore washer have reset button?

The washer does not have a reset button. The only method to reset the machine is by disconnecting it for 5-10 minutes and restarting the unit. If this does not work, then the problem could&nbsp; be with the main control board or the power cable.
