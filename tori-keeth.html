Professional Life

Talking about Keeth’s professional life, she made her acting debut at the young age of 13. She was cast in a Samsung commercial for the first time.

Likewise, in 2014, she made her appearance in Escaping the Prophet, a TLC series that tells the story of an ex-FLDS member who is committed to bringing down one of the most hazardous polygamist cults in the US.

In this shoe, she played a minor role in the sitcom ‘Speechless’. She co-starred alongside John Ross Bowie, Micah Fowler, and Minnie Driver, the drama series explores both the comic side and the severe challenges a family faces with their disabled family member.

Furthermore, she was cast in Henry Danger as a guest in 2018.  In the year 2019, she also starred as Sophie Walker in the movie The Message.
