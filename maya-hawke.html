Career

Maya Hawke started her career as a model with popular American fashion magazine Vogue following the footsteps of her mother and grandmother. After that, she has completed various modeling assignments and has modeled for many renowned fashion brands like AllSaints, Calvin Klein, and others.

In the year 2017, Maya Hawke made her acting debut with her appearance in the popular movie Little Women in which she played the lead role of Jo March. After that, she made her appearance in many popular movies like Ladyworld, Once Upon a Time in Hollywood, Human Capital, and several other popular movies.

The biggest breakthrough in her career came out when she made her appearance in the popular Netflix series Stranger Things in the third season in the year 2019. She played the role of Robin in the show and mostly shared the screen space with Joe Kerry’s character of Steve Harrington.

Along with her acting career in the year 2019, she started her singing career and released two singles titled To Love a Boy and Stay Open. After that in the year 2020, she gave her first solo live performance in New York City. On 18 March 2020, she released her debut album titled Blush along with a single titled By Myself.
