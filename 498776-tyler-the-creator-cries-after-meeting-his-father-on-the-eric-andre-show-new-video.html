Tyler, The Creator Cries After Meeting His &quot;Father&quot; On The Eric Andre Show

Eric Andre's Adult Swim talk show has been off-the-wall from the pilot, but the comedian (joined by his straight man sidekick, Hannibal Burress) has managed to up the absurdity pretty consistently over time.  

A couple weeks back we saw Wiz Khalifa stop by, maintaining his laid-back stoner vibe while chaos ensued around him. This week, Tyler, The Creator makes an appearance, and of course, he makes sure to bring his own bizarre energy to the mix.

The interview begins with Tyler pushing over Andre's desk, before being introduced to his "father", who happens to be a creepy white dude, but nonetheless brings the Odd Future leader to tears.
