Sarah-Jade Bleau - Age, Family, Bio

Popular TikTok star who is best known for the dance videos she posts to her sjbleau account. Her content has earned her over 12 million followers on the app. Her full name is Sarah-Jade Bleau. 

Before Fame

She launched her TikTok account in April 2020. 

Trivia

One of her most popular videos features her dancing like a robot. 

Family Life

Her dad, Jean-Francois, has appeared on her sjbleau TikTok account. She has a younger brother named Zack Bleau. She has been romantically linked to TikTok star Tony Lopez. 

Associated With

She posted a duet with TikTok stars Ondreaz and Tony Lopez in April 2020.
