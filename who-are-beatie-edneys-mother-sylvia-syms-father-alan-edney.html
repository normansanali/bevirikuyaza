Who Are Beatie Edneys Mother Sylvia Syms &amp; Father Alan Edney?

Actress Beatie Edney is the daughter of mother Sylvia Syms and father Alan Edney. Meet her parents on IG and also learn more about the actress below!

Beatie Edney is a Television actress.

She rose to fame through her character as Heather MacLeod in the 1986 film Highlander, the first entry in the Highlander series. She later returned to the role again in the 2000 film Highlander: Endgame.

In addition to that, her credit roles include a leading role in the 1986 television series Lost Empires, based on the novel by J.B. Priestley, in which she acted alongside Colin Firth.

Moreover, she has also made appearances in episodes of a host of successful British television dramas such as Rosemary & Thyme, A Touch of Frost, Prime Suspect, Inspector Morse (and its spin-off Lewis), Agatha Christie’s Poirot (episode The Mysterious Affair at Styles) and Wallander.

Furthermore, recently, she appeared as a guest in the “Love your weekend with Alan Titchmarsh” alongside Timothy West and David Domoney.

Who Are Beatie Edney’s Mother Sylvia Syms & Father Alan Edney?

Actress Beatie Edney was born to her mother Sylvia Syms and father Alan Edney in London, England, UK.

Slyvia Syms is a renowned actress. She is famed for her roles in the films Woman in a Dressing Gown, Ice Cold in Alex, No Trees in the Street, Victim, and The Tamarind Seed. She was perhaps one of the best actresses of her time. On the other hand, her father passed away due to a stroke, a day after he had a heart operation, according to the source.

The actress’s parents are however known to be divorced after thirty-three years of marriage. They got married in 1956 and divorced in 1989. The reason for their divorce is believed to be due to Alan’s infidelity. Per the source, he was having an affair with a woman called Anne, with whom he shared a daughter.

In addition to that, Beatie also has an elder brother who Sylvia adopted.

Meet Her Parents On IG

It appears that the actress’s parents are not active on Instagram.

However, her mother Slyvia is mostly featured in her Instagram posts. You can find Beatie on Instagram under the handle @beatieedney.

She has amassed 13.8 K followers and 1309 posts to date. On her Instagram grid, she frequently posts about her programs, movies, and her fun time with her mother. Besides, her posts on the platform exhibit her passion for dogs.

According to her bio, she describes herself as an ambassador of the Mayhew and DirtyGirls- the latter company works with refugee women to create a bag out of leftover fabrics used in TV and films.
