Meek Mill Reveals Joint Record Label Venture With Jay-Z: Dream Chasers

According to the latest reports, Meek Mill and Jay-Z have been fighting for the prison reform together, and now their collaboration expands in the music industry as well.

The Shade Room just reported that they just entered a partnership for a joint record label venture with Roc Nation.

Here’s Meek’s announcement on social media:

‘@DreamChasers Records x @rocnation official joint venture label “Black entrepreneur, nobody did us no favors…Nobody gave us sh*t, WE MADE US” – HOV ✍🏾💪🏾’ he captioned his post.

https://www.instagram.com/p/B0TIE_8AEaI/

Fans and followers congratulated the two in the comments section.

TSR writes that ‘The new label marks a turning point in Meek’s career, as he will now have experience on the business side of the music industry.’

They continue and reported that during a recent interview with CBS This Morning, Meek and Jay highlighted the importance of creating new roads in the music industry for those to come.

‘The music and the culture that we create, we’ve been giving it away for so long,’ Jay said.

He continued and stated that ‘We clean the floors up before we own the building, but we don’t shine shoes anymore.’
