Jennie Garth Shares Acting Advice for Daughter Luca Bella

Like mother, like daughter! Jennie Garth has given her eldest, Luca Bella, words of wisdom on the acting industry as the 22-year-old follows in her footsteps.

“I tell her to do the work,” the BH90210 alum, 47, told Us Weekly exclusively on Tuesday, December 10, while promoting Shipt.com and Sur La Table. “‘Don’t expect to just be famous. I got lucky, but you’ve got to do the work. You’ve got to put in the time.’”

The Illinois native, who also shares Lola, 17, and Fiona, 13, with ex-husband Peter Facinelli, admitted that watching Luca Bella step into acting has been “fun and scary at the same time.” Garth explains, “She’s so young and beautiful. I sit there and think, ‘Oh, my gosh. That was me however many years ago.’ But it’s interesting to watch it from a different perspective now and I’m glad that I’m there to guide her and advise her when she wants or needs it. I didn’t really have that other than my team, my manager or agent, at the time, but to have your mom have experienced similar things is pretty cool.”

The mother-daughter pair even costarred in Lifetime’s Your Family or Your Life, which premiered in November.

Acting isn’t the only thing that the two have in common. In fact, Garth and all of her daughters are “weirdly healthy eaters.“ She described their “grazing” snacking habits to Us on Tuesday, saying, “We’ll just come home and hang out in the kitchen and just eat hummus or whatever’s there. And that’s dinner sometimes, because we’re like, ‘Oh, I’m stuffed. I couldn’t eat dinner now.’”
