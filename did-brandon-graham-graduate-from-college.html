Did Brandon Graham graduate from college?

Brandon Lee Graham is an American football defensive end for the Philadelphia Eagles of the National Football League (NFL). He was drafted by the Eagles in the first round of the 2010 NFL Draft with the thirteenth selection in the draft and the first from the Big Ten Conference. He played college football at Michigan.

“Did Brandon Graham graduate from college?”

Yes he did, Before graduating from Detroit’s Crockett High School, Graham was named a U.S. Army All-American and Gatorade Player of the Year.

He accepted a scholarship offer from his home state college the University of Michigan among the many offers he received for his outstanding performances in high school.

Brandon was part of the Michigan Wolverines football team. After a taken a break from football in high school due to a suspension, he entered college with additional weight than was expected.

Graham was the 2009 Big Ten Conference co-MVP as recognized by the Chicago Tribune Silver Football award.
