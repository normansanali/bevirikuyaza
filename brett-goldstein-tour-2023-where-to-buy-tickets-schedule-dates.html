Brett Goldstein is crossing the pond.

The comedian, “Ted Lasso” star and two-time Emmy winner just added ten additional shows to his North American ‘Second Best Night Of Your Life Tour.’

He’s now set to play New York City’s Beacon Theatre on Friday, Nov. 10, Saturday, Nov. 11 and Sunday, Nov. 12 as part of the annual New York Comedy Festival.

Other stops on the run include gigs in Denver, Boston, Minneapolis, Sacramento and Portland.

“There will be swears,” the foul-mouthed comic promised on Instagram.

And if you want tickets to see Roy Kent kick it live onstage, we’re here to give you an assist.

Although inventory isn’t available on Ticketmaster until Friday, July 28, fans who want to ensure they have tickets ahead of time can purchase on sites like Vivid Seats before tickets are officially on sale.
