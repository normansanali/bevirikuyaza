African-American Bruce Bruce is an actor and stand-up comedian who has an estimated net worth of $3 million. Born Bruce Church on February 9, 1965 in Atlanta, Georgia, he grew up in one of the city’s roughest ghettos, The Bluff. He began his comedy career performing stand-up while cooking barbecue at his first job out of high school. He went on with performing impromptu comedy sets while working as a salesman for Frito-Lay. Eventually, he found his way to the Comedy Act Theatre in Atlanta.

With his performances, Bruce Bruce continued to be successful as a comedian that led to a chance to audition for BET’s Coast to Coast series. And as they always say, the rest is history. He has appeared in several stand-up comedy specials and television series. He was the host of BET’s Comic View and Coming to the Stage. He had appeared as part of HBO’s Def Comedy Jam and Showtime at the Apollo several times. He has been featured in Comedy Central Presents. He was included in the films such as The Wash, Hair Show, XXX: State of the Union, Larry the cable Guy: Health Inspector, Idlewild, Who’s Your Caddy?, and Think Like a Man. He has released multiple comedy DVDs of live performances. He was also a spokesperson for Popeye’s Chicken & Biscuits.
