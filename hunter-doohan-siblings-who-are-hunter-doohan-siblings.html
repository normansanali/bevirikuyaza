Hunter Doohan siblings: Who are Hunter Doohan siblings?

Hunter Doohan, born on January 19, 1994, in Fort Smith ( Arkansas ), is an American actor.

He is best known for his roles in the television series Truth Be Told: The Poison of Truth, Your Honor, and Wednesday.
Hunter Doohan was born in Fort Smith, Arkansas. As a teenager, he took part in various theater workshops at his high school as well as those in his town .

From 2019 to 2020, he played a recurring role on the Apple TV+ television series, Truth Be Told: The Poison of Truth. He plays the young Warren, played by adult Aaron Paul.

From 2020 to 2021, he portrays Adam Desiato in ten episodes of the television series Your Honor.

In 2022, he joined the cast of the television series Mercredi ( Wednesday ), inspired by The Addams Family comic books created by Charles Addams .

In June 2022, he shares on social networks his marriage to Fielder Jewwet, his longtime boyfriend.
