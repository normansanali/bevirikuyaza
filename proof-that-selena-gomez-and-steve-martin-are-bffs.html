7 Times Selena Gomez And Steve Martin Proved They Are Are BFFs

Selena Gomez has formed a great bond with her Only Murders in the Building costars Steve Martin and Martin Short. It’s no surprise that the Ice Cream singer had new-found friends when she starred in the Hulu series with Steve Martin and Martin Short. Aside from the huge paycheck for the stars of Only Murders in the Building, they seem to have earned long time pals as the trio formed such great bond. This friendship is so evident especially with Selena Gomez and Steve Martin interacting and bantering in their promotional interviews. Take a peek at their banters at different interviews.

7 Selena Gomez Taught Steve Martin Cardi B’s WAP

During an interview on BBC Radio 1 to promote Only Murders in the Building, it was revealed that Steve Martin only knew Cardi B’s song titled WAP was because the Heart Wants What It Wants singer has taught him the song. When Steve Martin and Martin Short were asked by the host Ali Plumb what was their Selena Gomez song, Martin said that it was the ‘splish, splash, I was taking a bath’ song which was the song Gomez sang during her formative Barney years. Short on the hand answered that she loved Gomez’s cover of W-A-P to which Selena said that it’s WAP and not W-A-P. Martin added that Selena was the one who introduced them to the song.
