Demi Lovato on Being Friends with Ariana Grande

  • Demi Lovato discusses the solid friendship they have with Ariana Grande in a new interview with Harper's BAZAAR.
  • The "I Love Me" singer describes their pairing as "supportive."
  • "The whole world seems to want to pit women against each other," Lovato says.

Demi Lovato has been in the entertainment industry for a long time, and they've made some firm friends along the way.

Opening up in a new interview with Harper's BAZAAR, Lovato explains why their friendship with fellow singer Ariana Grande continues to work so well. Lovato says, "I love the fact that Ariana and I have such a supportive friendship because it's hard to find."
