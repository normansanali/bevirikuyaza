Introduction

From grocery store clerk to millionaire entrepreneur and real estate genius, Tom Ferry has learned the real estate business back-to-front and seeks to help others to do the same.

The Origin Story

Tom Ferry was growing up on a path that could have led to prison or worse.

After his father kicked him out of the house, Tom found himself living in an apartment in the ghetto with a bullet hole in the window.

He dropped out of school, got his GED, and started bagging groceries at the local grocery store.

Tom knew that he wanted to someday be successful, but he had no idea how to go about achieving it.

His father, Mike Ferry, was massively successful in the real estate industry, and Tom knew he wanted to follow in his father’s footsteps.

When Mike Ferry entered the grocery store where Tom worked, he approached his father and expressed his desire to go into business with him.

Tom Ferry started at the bottom of his father’s company, working in the mail room, and worked his way up.

Tom learned every aspect of the real estate business, from logistics to sales to product creation.

Tom also adopted his father’s passion for motivational material.

He claimed that he listened to every motivational coach he possibly could as he strived toward bettering himself.

Tom had a goal to run his father’s business within five years and within nine he had achieved just that.

He learned his business skills by building his father’s company and then went out on his own.

Career

Tom Ferry’s father was a real estate entrepreneur and gave him his start in the real estate industry.

By 2005, Tom Ferry had started his own competitive coaching company in Irvine, CA, Tom Ferry – Your Coach.

This company hosts events, conferences, and mentorships for real estate agents.

He also personally trains other coaches and agents.

Tom has been listed as one of the most powerful people in residential real estate.

He has also contracted several agents who have applied his system and have made millions as coaches through his business.

Ferry has embraced social media and review-based platforms from early on, knowing they would eventually be useful for business. He also established high-profile partnerships with billion-dollar companies.

Tom started an investment company that invests in promising real estate companies to drive the industry forward.

Books

Tom Ferry released his first novel “Life! By Design” which he co-wrote with Laura Morton in 2010, and it became a New York Times bestseller that year.

This novel tackled the issue of addictions.

His second book was released in 2017 and quickly became an Amazon bestseller that same year. This novel was a guidebook for real estate professionals.
