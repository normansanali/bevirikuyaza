Early Life

Jennifer Aniston, originally Jennifer Joanna Aniston, was born on February the 11th, 1969, in Los Angeles, California, around the Sherman Oaks neighborhood.

Both of her parents were actors. Her father was John Aniston, and her mother was Nancy Dow.

Her mother was of English, Scottish, and Irish, and also of Greek descent; however, her father’s ancestry was from the Greek island of Crete.

Aniston has two half-brothers, John Melick and Alex Aniston. They moved to New York City in her early childhood days.

He parents got divorced when she was just nine years of age. Aniston went to Waldorf School at the age of six.

She discovered acting at the age of 11 at her school.

Afterward, she attended Manhattan’s Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School of Music & Art and Performing Arts, where she joined the school’s drams.
