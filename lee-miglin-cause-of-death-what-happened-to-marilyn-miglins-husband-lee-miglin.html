Lee Miglin Cause Of Death: What happened to Marilyn Miglins husband Lee Miglin?

Another murder, the violent shooting of Lee Miglin, a self-made real-estate magnate, was making national headlines two months before Andrew Cunanan killed Gianni Versace.

Cunanan’s third murder in a spree that spanned Minneapolis to Miami was not immediately linked to him by authorities.

Despite this, the real-estate developer’s wealth, his status as a generous society fixture alongside his Home Shopping Network empress wife, Marilyn Miglin, and the murder’s unusual circumstances drew widespread media attention.

Miglin was assassinated on May 4 in Chicago’s most affluent neighborhood, at the home he shared with his wife while she was out of town on business.

The cause of his death is multiple stab wounds

The Chicago Tribune reported that Miglin’s body “was discovered in a detached garage, tucked under a car and obscured by a trash can.

Miglin’s feet were bound together and his face was carefully wrapped in masking tape, except for a hole for his nose, sources said.

The masking tape was soaked in blood, as were Miglin’s shoulders and chest, sources said.”
