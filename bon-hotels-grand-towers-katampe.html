Bon Hotels Grand Towers, Katampe

The Bon Hotels Grand Towers is a luxury hotel situated at Plot 1139 Cadastral Zone.

The Bon Hotels Grand Towers is located in Katampe, Abuja.

The Bon Hotels Grand Towers has classy rooms with a classy feel and polished with modern furnishing. Room categories include the Standard Room, Executive Room, Diplomatic Room and Presidential Room.

Each room features a 24/7 electricity, flat screen TV sets, air conditioners, single beds, and study desks.

A number of facilities are available at the Bon Hotels Grand Towers. These include a restaurant, swimming pool, fitness facilities, and a bar.

The hotel offers additional services on requests such as 24-hour front desk, Event halls, Taxi pick-up, Spa services, Elevator, Gift shop, Electric fence, Luggage storage, and Express check-in/check-out.
