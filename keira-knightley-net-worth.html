Keira Knightley Career

Since childhood, Knightley has already appeared in smaller television roles and advertisement commercials.

She then appeared in television movies like The Treasure Seekers (1996), Innocent Lies (1995), Coming Home (1998), and Oliver Twist (1999).

Knightley was cast in the epic Star Wars: Episode I- The Phantom Menace (1999) as Sabe, who was handmaiden and a decoy planted for Padme Amidala.

Knightley appeared in mainstream movies since the 2002 British movie Bend it Like Beckham.

The movie was an instant hit garnering over $77 million in box office worldwide, boosting her career.

She then worked alongside Johnny Depp in the commercially hit movie Pirates of the Caribbean series.

She has also appeared in several well-known movies like Atonement, Pride & Prejudice, Never Let Me Go, Anna Karenina, and The Imitation Game, etc.

Keira Knightley Awards and Nominations

Knightley has been nominated over 94 times in several Film Awards, and among them, she has been able to win 24.

The major highlight of her nomination was to be nominated in the Best Actress category for Pride and Prejudice in 2005 and in Best Supporting Actress for The Imitation Game in 2014.

She was further nominated for the same category of best actress and supporting actress in British Academy Film Awards, Golden Globe Awards, and Screen Actors Guild Awards.

She won the London Film Critics award for best newcomer in the movie Bend it Like Beckham and Variety Award at British Independent Film Awards.
