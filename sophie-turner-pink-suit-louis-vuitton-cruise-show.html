Sophie Turner Pops in Bubblegum-Pink Pantsuit at Louis Vuitton Show

On Thursday, the Louis Vuitton 2025 Cruise Show took place at a venue in the vibrant Park Güell in Barcelona, and celebrity friends of the brand filled the front row. Among them was Game of Thrones alum Sophie Turner, who embraced the whimsical setting by sporting her own eye-popping look.

For the presentation, Turner showed up in bubblegum-pink pantsuit courtesy of the fashion house. But it wasn’t your regular suit, as it seemed to be leaning into the country-western trend being touted by everyone from Rihanna to Bella Hadid as of late.

Europa Press News//Getty Images

All About Sophie Turner:

All across the set there were winks to the rodeo aesthetic—from the visible stitching to the large pockets and silver studs dotting the front of the blazer. Not to mention, Turner wore a skinny oxblood leather belt with a large gold buckle in the front, which only leaned further into the cowboy theme.

Louis Vuitton/Instagram//Instagram

To complete the look, the Emmy-nominated star wore a pair of white platform heels with metallic silver bands decorating the front, and she carried a matching white micro bag. For beauty, the actor painted her nails the same color as her belt, and she rocked gorgeous blonde mid-length waves.

It was no major surprise to see Turner in attendance at the Louis Vuitton show, given that she’s had a long relationship with the French fashion house and been an ambassador of the brand for years. She showed up to the label’s Fall 2024 runway show back in March, where she sported a crimson top with draping off-the-shoulder sleeves, which she tucked into striped pants held up with black suspenders. However, the real highlight of that look was a pair of asymmetrical shoes that were half thong sandal, half mule.
