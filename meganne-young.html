Meganne Young Biography

Megan Lynne Talde Young-Daez was born Megan Lynne Talde Young-Daez on February 27, 1990. She was born in the United States, in the city of Alexandria. She is of English, Scottish, German, Filipino, Welsh, and Northern Irish descent and holds dual citizenship in the United States and the Philippines. Her father, Calvin Young III, and mother, Victoria Young, gave birth to her. Lauren Young has four siblings: Aiden Young, Calvin C Young IV, and Victor Young. Her younger sister, Lauren Young, is a well-known actress and model.

When Young was ten years old, she and her family relocated to Castillejos, Zambales. She attended Regional Science High School III in Subic Bay FreeportZone from 2002 to 2005. Young finished in the top six of the second season of the talent reality show StarStruck in 2004. She then gets her first acting job in “Love to Love Presents: Love Ko Urok” on the GMA Network. In the series, she played Meg, a character. She later starred as Anna in Asian Treasures and as a Fairy in Mga Kuwento ni Lola Basyang. In 2011, she relocated to Metro Manila to pursue her acting career and enrolled at De La Salle-College of Saint Benilde to study filmmaking.

Career

Meganne spent more than two years at the GMA Artist Center before joining ABS-CBN with her sister Lauren. She made her television debut on ABS-Magic CBN’s Presents’Astigs and Abt Ur. Luv, a youth-oriented show. She later played Shane with Zanjoe Marudo in the film Kokey. She rose to prominence after appearing as one of the celebrity housemates in Pinoy Big Brother: Celebrity Edition 2. Her big break came in 2012 when she landed the starring role in Hiyas as Sapphire Salvador. She’s also been a host on a number of Studio 23 shows.

Young competed in the Miss World Philippines pageant in 2013, winning the title and being crowned on August 19, 2013, on the contest’s coronation night. Shortly later, she was named Miss World 2013 in Bali, Indonesia. Young is the first Filipina to win the Miss World beauty pageant. During the preliminary rounds, she also placed second in the “People’s Champion,” fourth in the “Multimedia Challenge,” and fifth in the “Beach Beauty” event. She also came out on top in the “Top Model” competitions. She was also given the title of Continental Queen of Beauty as Miss World Asia 2013. Young appeared as a guest in the Dances of the World episode of the final season of the show.

Meganne Young Husband, Marriage

Meganne Young is married to Mikael Daez. He is a well-known advertising model, actor, news anchor, and vlogger. The couple started dating in 2011, but it wasn’t until 2017 that they openly declared their romance in an interview with Pep. Young began dating for the first time when she was 16 years old, although little information about the relationship is accessible.
