Al Roker Ex-Wife: Meet Alice Bell

Alice Bell was a WNBC producer and the ex-wife of Al Roker a weather forecaster, journalist, television personality, and author from the United States

Roker married Alice Bell in December 1984. He and Bell adopted daughter Courtney as an infant in 1987. Roker and Bell later divorced. There is no information about Alice Bell aside from her marriage to Al Roker.

Al Roker is best known as the long-time weatherman on NBC’s “The Today Show,” where he also co-hosts “3rd Hour Today.” From 2009 to 2015, he hosted The Weather Channel’s “Wake Up with Al” morning show.

Roker’s “Rokerthon” events have set several Guinness World Records, and the Today Plaza was renamed “Rokerfeller Plaza” in 2018 to honor Al’s 40 years with NBC.

Roker has written 12 books, including “Never Going Back: Winning the Weight Loss Battle Forever” (2014), “Al Roker’s Extreme Weather: Tornadoes, Typhoons, and Other Weather Phenomena” (2017), and “Ruthless Tide: The Heroes and Villains of America’s Astonishing Gilded Age Disaster” (2018).
