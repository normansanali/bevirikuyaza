Diamond Platnumz Announces FOA World Tour

Highly rated Tanzanian bongo flava recording artist and music executive, Nasibu Abdul Juma Issack, widely known as Diamond Platnumz has announced a world tour.

The multiple award winning singer, songwriter, entertainer, philanthropist and performing artiste has taken to his official social media page to announce a world tour dubbed; ‘FOA World Tour’.

The ‘FOA’ World tour will see the WCB Wasafi headline act perform in over fifteen different countries across the globe. Diamond Platnumz will perform in countries like United Kingdom, Canada, Germany, Finland, just to mention a few.

The ongoing ‘FOA’ World Tour which kicked-off on Saturday, May, 7 2022 in Ethiopia will end in Sierra Leone on Saturday, November 26, 2022 in. Diamond has hinted of updating fans and music lovers of more additional dates and cities. See flyer below.

Diamond Platnumz is currently promoting his new work “Ramadhan” featuring Mbosso and Ricardo Momo which was released on Friday, April 8, 2022. His 10-tracked studio album, ‘First Of All’ has amassed over 50 million streams on Boomplay.
