Rising Australian TikTok Star Caitlyn Loane Dies at 19

Caitlyn Loane has sadly passed away.

The 19-year-old rising social media star, who amassed a huge following on TikTok for her farming and agriculture-related content, died at the age of 19, her family confirmed Thursday (July 8).

Click inside to read more…

“She was a lovely, crazy young woman who was an invaluable member of our family. Words can’t describe our loss,” her father Phillip Loane said to The Mercury.

The family declined to provide specifics about the cause of her death, but her father said: “every day should be ‘R U OK?’ day.”

“Her smile lit up the room. She wasn’t afraid to get her hands dirty—in some photos she has mud up to her knees,” said her mother Richele Loane to the outlet.

She was considered a trailblazer for women in farming, especially in her state of Tasmania. She was also a fourth-generation farmer who followed in her dad’s footsteps.

Our thoughts are with Caitlyn Loane‘s loved ones at this difficult time. We have sadly lost all of these stars in 2021.
