Savannah Marshall Net Worth

Savannah Marshall has a total net worth of $2 million which she has made over the years through her thriving career in boxing. Savannah has held the WBO female middleweight title since 2020.

Savannah Marshall hails from Hartlepool in the North East of England. Savannah Marshall started boxing when she was 12 years old and had joined a local Hartlepool Headland club.

Savannah Marshall remains the only boxer ever to have defeated the middleweight women’s gold medallist Claressa Shields, in the 2012 Olympics. Savannah Marshall fights out of the orthodox stance.

During her amateur career, Savannah Marshall won at the 2012 AIBA Women’s World Boxing Championships in Qinhuangdao, China.

Savannah Marshall got signed by Floyd Mayweather as a professional in 2017. She made her professional boxing debut at super-middleweight on the undercard of The Money Fight, in the professional boxing super-fight between undefeated five-division champion Floyd Mayweather Jr. and the former UFC Lightweight Champion and Featherweight Champion Conor McGregor.
