Johnny Depps Wild Style Evolution: His Craziest Fashion Moments

Johnny Depp hit the scene in the ‘80s rocking t-shirts, leather jackets and funky hats – he instantly became one of Hollywood’s newest heartthrobs. Over the years, his fashion has evolved, but he has stayed in tune with his uniquely whimsical style. Some may even describe it more like a pirate-rocker hybrid (all the scarfs!).

Throughout the years he’s fashionably worn suits — sometimes in cold colors like bright yellow — vests and of course leather jackets. But a big part of Depp’s style is his accessories. The man can drastically change his entire ensemble with the addition of a few finishing touches. Throughout the years you can see his love for hats, scarfs and sunglasses, each a vital part of his look.
