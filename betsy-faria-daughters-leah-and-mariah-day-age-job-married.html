Who Are Betsy Faria Daughters, Leah and Mariah Day? Age, Job, Married

Meet Betsy Faria’s daughters, Leah and Mariah Day. After NBC premiered the show “The Thing About Pam” viewers wondered what their daughters have been up to after the show. In this article, you’ll learn about their age, their job, and their marital status or dating life.

So, scroll down this article to learn more about them in this Betsy Faria Daughters article.

Who Are Betsy Faria Daughters?

Late Betsy Faria had two daughters named Leah and Mariah Day at the time of her death. She had both of them from their previous relationship. At the time of her death, two sisters were convinced that their step-father Russ Faria had killed their mother.

Meet Leah Day, Betsy Faria’s Oldest Daughter

On the evening of her mother’s death, Leah the oldest daughter was going to U.S. Cellular to upgrade her phone plan. She said that Betsy had promised to authorize charges over the phone. She called her mom from the store at 7:21, 7:26, and 7:30, and Betsy did not answer.

During the trial, Leah also testified that Russ and her mother fought often. “It wasn’t The Brady Bunch,” Leah said.

In January 2012, Leah wrote on her Facebook, “I can’t help but have these random break downs….. I miss you mom sooooo much and all I do is want to hear your voice again… I had a dream with you in it last night and it felt soooooo real… only if I can have dreams like that every night I love you sooo much mom!!!!”.

  • Leah Day Age

On 13 September 2021, Leah Day turned 31 years old.

  • Leah Day Job

Leah Day is currently working three jobs. She works at Silver Pancake House which she started her job there on 11 January 2022. She is also working at 5 Star Burgers – St. Louis as a server. Furthermore, she started working at First Watch as a server in 2017.

Before that, Leah had worked for the Former Server at 54th Street Grill & Bar from 2016 to 2017. Whereas, she also worked at Chevy’s as a server from 2014 to 2016.

Leah graduated high school from Timberland High School. She attended Healing Arts Center where she studied massage therapy and graduated in 2017. Furthermore, she also attended Current Trends Academy where she studied cosmetology.

  • Is Leah Day Married?

No, Leah Day doesn’t seem to be married but her Facebook revealed that she is in a relationship as of March 2022. She is currently dating Chris Casmier (@chris.casmier). Leah has been dating her man at least since February 2020, the first time they appeared together.

In May 2020, Leah wrote on her Facebook, “I’m loving you!!!” while posting a close-up picture of her and her partner. Based on her posts, he is the father of one daughter. Speaking more about her man, he is from University City, Missouri. He attended University City Senior High.

Meet Mariah Day, Betsy Faria’s Youngest Daughter

Mariah Day is the youngest daughter of Betsy Faria. Mariah recently wrote on her Facebook, “I refuse to continuously remember my mom like the way she died and all the horrific events that happened after. So let’s honor her memory by remembering who she was. The fighter, the spontaneous go-getter, the traveler, the hands-on mother, the dancing queen and so much more. She was extraordinary beyond her death story and she deserved/s so much better.”

At the time of her mother’s death, Mariah was only 17 so one could only imagine how traumatic it was for her to lose her mother the way she did.

  • Mariah Day Age

Born on 28 March 1994, as of March 2022, Mariah Day is 27 years old.

  • Mariah Day Job

Mariah Day is currently working as a server at Five Star Burger. She has been working there since March 2018. From 2015 to 2018, Mariah worked at 54th Street Grill & Bar as a bartender in Chesterfield, Missouri. Starting from 2019, Mariah has also been attending St. Louis Community College where she studies Human Services/Social Work.

But, Mariah also has made it her mission to speak about wrongful conviction. She makes TikTok videos under her account (@justiceforbetsy). She has uploaded two videos as of this writing. In the first video, she talked about her mother getting murdered and accusing the system of wrongfully convicting her stepfather which has ruined their relationship. (https://musclemx.com/)

  • Is Mariah Day Married?

No, Mariah Day isn’t married but she is also in a relationship and has one child with her partner Derrick. She welcomed her son Elijah in May 2019. She admires her baby father for being a great dad on her socials.

In June 2020, Mariah wrote on her Facebook, “Anyone can be a father, but it takes someone special to be a dad. I love watching him be the best dad to Elijah. We are so lucky. Here are a few photos of Derek’s dadding out. (He trims Elijah’s nails indefinitely because I had one bad experience.) Happy Father’s Day to all you awesome dads, uncles, grandpas, step-dads, father figures.” Whereas, as of March 2022, their second child (daughter) is on the way.”

In August 2021, Mariah had updated, “Eli is not sure what to think. But I am sure he is going to be the best big brother! My due date is March 24th, my mom’s birthday.”
