The Rock Teases WWE Return

It is safe to say that Hollywood megastar Dwayne ‘The Rock’ Johnson is one of the greatest WWE Superstars of all time. Following his success outside the Stamford-based promotion, even a guest appearance on Raw or SmackDown goes viral.

A few weeks ago, the former WWE World Heavyweight Champion showed up on an episode of SmackDown. That became the most social moment in WWE history by crossing over 100 Million views across all social media platforms. That moment seems to have been broken by the return of CM Punk.

It looks like The Rock wants to create another viral moment because he teased another return to the Stamford-based promotion.

Recently, The People’s Champion shared a post on Instagram about him fulfilling a Make-A-Wish kid’s wish. While sharing the post he also teased that he will be making a return to lay the SmackDown on all other wrestler’s candy asses.

“Droppin that old school Rock theme music for my boy Jayden Harden from @makeawishamerica! Jayden is 15 and he’s awesome!! Jayden’s wish was to meet, “the people’s champ”. He is a HUUUUUGE ROCK & pro wrestling fan and we had a BLAST chopping up his favorite wrestlers and matches!! I LOVE pro wrestling so my time with Jayden was super dope! ??? I told him a little secret ? that may or may not involve some dude returning to WWE to lay the SmackDown on all their candy asses ??? There’s a lot of negative and toxic noise out there in the world – but there’s a lot of positive, good things too, and Jayden is one of them. #MakeAWishDay #21Kids #BestDayEver,” The Rock posted.

With the Royal Rumble coming up, maybe we see him at the PLE.
