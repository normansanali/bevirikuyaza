Sonny Side - Age, Family, Bio

Creator of the comedy/food review show and YouTube channel Best Ever Food Review Show who explores the culinary world of each country. He earned 8 million subscribers on YouTube, but also brought his show to Facebook to earn an additional 900,000 followers. Some of his most popular videos have gone to earn over 20 million views.

Before Fame

He began posting video content on both YouTube and Facebook in December of 2015.

Trivia

He said that he didn't try a fresh peach until he was in his 20s. He was offered a full-time role as a video producer while traveling to Vietnam. He has gained over 1.7 billion views on his videos.

Family Life

His real name is Will Sonbuchner. He has resided in Korea and Vietnam.

Associated With

He posted a video in 2017 called "Sonny Side VS Andrew Zimmern - Stinky Tofu."
