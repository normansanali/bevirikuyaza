Enola Holmes Cast &amp; character ( 2020 Movie): Everything You Need To Know

Enola Holmes, a Netflix original series starring Millie Bobby Brown as the younger sister of Sherlock Holmes, also features Henry Cavill, Helena Bonham Carter, Sam Claflin, and other notable actors.

Enola, played by Millie in the novel, sets out to find her mother after she vanishes on the morning of her sixteenth birthday.

Enola’s conservative older brothers want to enroll their sister in a school for ‘decent’ young girls, but she becomes involved in a larger-than-expected mystery, requiring her amateur detective talents.

Millie Bobby Brown played Enola Holmes

The sister of Sherlock Holmes who looks into her mother’s absence is played by Millie Bobby Brown in the movie Enola, which has the same name. In addition to playing Madison Russell in Godzilla: King of the Monsters, Brown is most recognized for her role as Eleven in Stranger Things. She will return to the role for the sequel Godzilla vs. Kong in 2021.

Mycroft Holmes Is Played By Sam Claflin

Sam Claflin also stars as Mycroft, the mustachioed brother of Enola and the second-most-frequently played Holmes on-screen to date. In addition to playing Finnick Odair in The Hunger Games series after his breakthrough role in Pirates of the Caribbean: On Stranger Tides, Sam Claflin most recently portrayed Oswald Mosley in Peaky Blinders season 5.

Sherlock Holmes is portrayed by

Henry plays Sherlock Holmes, Enola’s brother, and you may have seen him in Man of Steel and Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice.

According to reports, Henry’s character in Enola Holmes displays a “more emotional” aspect of the standard Sherlock Holmes.

Playing Eudoria Holmes is

Eudoria, played by Helena Bonham Carter, is Enola’s mother, and her absence initiates the main conflict. In Sweeney Todd: The Demon Barber of Fleet Street, directed by her ex-husband Tim Burton, Carter played Mrs. Lovett. In Fight Club, she also played Marla Singer, and in The Crown, she presently plays Queen Margaret.
