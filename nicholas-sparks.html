Biography

Nicholas Sparks was born in Omaha, NE on December 31, 1965. American romantic author whose novels A Walk To Remember and The Notebook have both been adapted into films. He published nearly twenty books between 1996 and 2013 and also worked as a screenwriter and producer. His novel, The Last Song, was adapted into a film starring Liam Hemsworth. He was his high school’s 1984 valedictorian and received a track and field scholarship to the University of Notre Dame. Following his college graduation, he worked a string of odd jobs, including real estate agent and waiter.
