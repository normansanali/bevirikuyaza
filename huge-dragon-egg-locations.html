Palworld Huge Dragon Egg Locations

Not sure where to find Huge Dragon Eggs in Palworld? Palworld features a massive open world for players to explore, collect resources, battle enemies, and find new Pals to add to their collection. However, finding the right Pals you need can be a difficult process. In this guide, we provide potential Huge Dragon Egg locations in Palworld. Furthermore, we explain how to hatch them and which Pals can hatch from them.

Palworld Huge Dragon Egg Locations

Where to Find Huge Dragon Egg Locations in Palworld

In Palworld, there are two ways of acquiring a Pal. One is to find and catch them randomly in the game’s vast open world. Another is to get them through breeding or finding a Pal egg in the wild. As you explore the world, you find these eggs from time to time, which you can then place into the Egg Incubator in order to Hatch them. And while most types of Eggs have random spawn locations, there is still a certain rule in regard to where they spawn. Namely, each egg has a dedicated habitat or biome where they spawn. So, where can you find a Huge Dragon Egg in Palworld?

Based on various reports in the community, the most likely place to find a Large Dragon Egg is Mount Obsidian. Specifically, Reddit user Namu342 mentions they have found one Huge Dragon Egg at the Fisherman Village on Obsidian Mountain. If you don’t know where that is, Mount Obsidian is straight west from the starting area.

In addition, other players report that you can find these eggs around the Ruined Fortress City. “Found it a Ruined Fortress City on top of a pillar close by to the teleport,” says Reddit user HeavenLeighVtuber. Check out their map below for the exact location. In general, it seems that your best chances to find Huge Dragon Eggs are at higher altitudes, particularly in the POIs around the Obsidian Mountain. Hence, if you want one, make sure to check out these locations! And if you don’t know how to hatch them, check out our Incubator Egg guide.
