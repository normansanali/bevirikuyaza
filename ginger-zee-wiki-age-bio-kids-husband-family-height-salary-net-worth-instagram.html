Ginger Zee Biography – Ginger Zee Wiki

Ginger Zee is an American television personality. Zee is Good Morning America’s chief meteorologist, reporting on the nation’s weather throughout the morning broadcast. Previously she was weather anchor for the weekend edition of Good Morning America. She also reports across all ABC News broadcasts and digital platforms. Zee attended Rockford High School in Rockford, Michigan, where she graduated in 1999. Zee attended Valparaiso University, where she obtained a Bachelor of Science degree in meteorology as well as a major in both mathematics and Spanish.

After she graduated from college, Zee served at various media outlets such as WEYI-TV in Flint, Michigan, WYIN-TV in Merrillville, Indiana, WLAV-FM and WOOD-TV both in Grand Rapids, Michigan, WBMA-LD ABC 33/40 in Birmingham, Alabama as an intern under chief meteorologist James Spann, and WMAQ in Chicago, Illinois, an NBC owned and operated station.
Zee come to the limelight after joining Good Morning America Weekend on November 12, 2011. Zee occasionally appears on other ABC programs such as Nightline and ABC World News Tonight. Since joining ABC News, Zee has covered major weather events and dozens of historic storms. She covered the devastated Jersey Shore during Hurricane Sandy, the Colorado floods and wildfires, and covered the wreckage from tornados in Moore and El Reno, Oklahoma. In 2013, she covered extreme weather conditions, ranging from the Boston blizzard to the record-breaking heat in Death Valley.
