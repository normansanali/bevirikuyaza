Allison Mack News, Photos and Videos Hollywood Life

Bio:
Allison Mack (born Allison Christin Mack in Preetz, West Germany on July 29, 1982) is an American actress. She began acting as a child and appeared on shows like Opposite Sex and Hiller and Diller, but is best known for the 10 years she spent playing Chloe Sullivan on the CW hit Smallville. This role led to two spinoff miniseries, Vengeance Chronicles and Chloe Chronicles, as well as other TV roles, the most recent being Lost in Oz. She made major headlines in 2018 when she was arrested by the FBI for her involvement with NXIVM, an alleged sex-trafficking cult. She has most recently been released on $5 million bail and is facing 15 years to life.

Personal Life:
Allison announced her engagement to her boyfriend Peter in 2003 after five years of dating, but called things off shortly after. She dated Chad Krowchuck, her Frog co-star, in 2008, then Sam Witwer, her Smallville co-star, in 2010. Although she and Sam got engaged three years later, they also split before tying the knot. Since this break-up, Allison has allegedly married actress Nicki Clyne, also a member of NXIVM, so the Canadian could stay in the U.S.
