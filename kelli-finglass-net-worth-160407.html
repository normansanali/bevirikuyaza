Kelli Finglass Net Worth

Kelli Finglass was born on December 30, 1965 in Texas and is best known for her role as the director of the Dallas Cowboys Cheerleaders. She was a cheerleader for the team for six seasons from 1984 to 1989 before taking on the role of director in 1991. She also produced the reality show Dallas Cowboys Cheerleaders: Making the Team on CMT.

About

Became the Director of Dallas Cowboys Cheerleaders in 1991 after previously serving as a Cheerleader for six seasons, from 1984 to 1989. She produced Dallas Cowboys Cheerleaders: Making the Team, a reality show on CMT.

Before Fame

She attended Texas Christian University and studied Modern Dance. She later attended the University of North Texas and received a degree in international marketing.

Trivia
