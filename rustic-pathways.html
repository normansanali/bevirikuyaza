Rustic Pathways Programs &amp; Reviews

Why Choose Rustic Pathways?

Since 1983, Rustic Pathways has empowered positive change, fostering impactful friendships and unforgettable travel experiences for teens aged 12-22. Join animal lovers, environmental activists, and explorers on adventures designed to ignite your passions, grow in confidence, and foster global awareness. There is a Rustic Pathways experience for every kind of student. In addition to high school summer experiences, Rustic Pathways offers spring break, college, and Young Explorer programs for those travelers not yet in high school. We offer diverse programs catering to different aspirations, from first-time travel experiences to deep immersion and service-oriented initiatives geared toward future changemakers. We offer programs in over 20 different countries across the United States, Central and South America, Europe, Asia, Africa, and the South Pacific. With us, students engage with the people and places they visit, forming genuine connections with host families, communities, program leaders, and fellow travelers. They return home with more than just photos and souvenirs. Past Rustic Pathways travelers will be the first to tell you that the people they met are what made their experience memorable.
