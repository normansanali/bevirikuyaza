Jennifer Aniston Bared Butt Before Kim Kardashian: I Was the Original

Sorry Kim! Nearly 20 years before Kim Kardashian‘s Paper magazine cover broke the Internet, Jennifer Aniston was a derriere-flashing trailblazer one the cover of Rolling Stone in March 1996.

Reminicising to Extra at the Hollywood premiere of Horrible Bosses 2 on Thursday, Nov. 20, Aniston boasted that her bare butt appeared “way” before Kardashian’s. “Oh, way, yeah. I was an original,” the star, 45, said.

“[Mine] was just innocent, there’s nothing aggressive about that,” Justin Theroux‘s fiancée added of her arguably tasteful nude in-bed cover, which was shot by Mark Seliger. “Sorry, Kim K.,” she jokingly added after Extra called her a “pioneer.”

Aniston’s cheeky 1996 RS cover marked her first solo cover for the magazine. The actress had previously posed for the mag with her four Friends costars in 1995.
