Sara Rue's Baby Daughter Talulah Has Traveled to London, Spain

Baby frequent flier! Sara Rue's daughter, Talulah, is just over six months old, and already the tot is racking up her share of frequent flier airline miles. "It's been really fun," Malibu Country's Rue, 34, told Us Weekly of motherhood at Project Angel Food's Angel Awards Aug. 11 in Los Angeles. "We've travelled. We took her to London, and I was a bridesmaid in my friend's wedding [in Spain] so we took her to Majorca."

Vowing to take the first six months off post-pregnancy from work to bond with Talulah, Rue tells Us she's in no immediate rush to get back in front of the cameras. "I'm starting to think about what I'll do next. I'm such a slacker," the busy new mom joked to Us. (Rue's Malibu Country, costarring Reba McEntire, was canceled in May 2013.)

Instead, Rue is soaking up the moments with her little girl. "She's sitting up and starting to blow bubbles," Rue beams of Talulah's recent milestones. "The first forming of the word 'mom' are coming any day now. She's a total angel baby."

As her daughter stays active, so does Rue, who has begun the process of slimming to her pre-baby weight. "It's a slow crawl back," reasons Rue, who told Us in March that she wasn't looking for a "hot bod" and instead just wanted to be a healthy role model for her daughter.

Making the weight loss journey easier for the actress is Paleta's Fit Mommy meal plan, which offers farm-to-table meal delivery options. "Their meals are very normal. It's just clean, healthy food and it's working," raves Rue. "It's like anything else [with weight loss], it works when you work it."
