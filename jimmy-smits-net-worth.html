Jimmy L. Smits Wiki Biography

Jimmy Smits is an American actor known for his roles as attorney Victor Sifuentes on the 1980s legal drama L.A. Law, as NYPD Detective Bobby Simone on the 1990s police
drama NYPD Blue, and as Matt Santos on The West Wing. He is also notable for his portrayal of Bail Organa in the Star Wars prequel trilogy, and Miguel Prado in Dexter.
In 2012, he joined the main cast of Sons of Anarchy as high-level pimp Nero Padilla.
